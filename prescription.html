The New Prescription Management System
December 27th, 2020
One of Pontem Medicalâ€™s key and foundational values is excellence. In everything we do, we are constantly striving to improve and be the best at what we do. This year, the Pontem Medical team decided that an expansion to the core services we offer to our customers would be the best way to do so. Now, in May, we are happy to reveal this brand new service - the prescription management system.

Obviously, many people have prescriptions for medications that they regularly take. As a doctor prescribes more and more medications, it can become confusing for patients to track what medications they take, when to take them, when to pick prescriptions up from the pharmacy, and more. On the other hand, a doctor might need to review all the prescribed medications their patient is taking, and that information may not be accessible.

With this new prescription management system, we have added a level of convenience and utility that eliminates this issue. Now, from the same portal that patients schedule appointments and attend virtual conferences with their doctor, they can easily view all of their prescriptions and associated important info. Doctors are also able to view this information for all of their patients from their own portal.
