Pontem Medical's services enable you to launch your organization into the future. We combine the cutting edge technologies of today with traditional medical practices to offer an excellent experience for both patient and provider.
Virtual Appointments
Our virtual appointment system allows for safe and convenient contact-free communication between patient and doctor. Doctors are largely able to conduct a checkup or consultation as usual, discussing the patient’s health, noting any symptoms or issues, and deciding what further action should be taken.
Scheduling System
With our virtual appointments acting as Pontem Medical's core and foundational service, our scheduling system serves to take these virtual appointments to the next level. From their personal device, patients can quickly check their doctor’s availability by time and date and book an appointment, noting any reasons or concerns. This appointment will instantly be uploaded to the doctor’s office’s systems. Both patient and doctor have the ability to link their smartphones to receive notifications of updates or changes for added convenience.
Diagnosis Questionnaire
Pontem Medical’s diagnosis questionnaire is a highly innovative and effective new service. As patients complete the questionnaire, they report issues, symptoms, or other notable circumstances regarding their health. Based on these responses, our questionnaire’s system will automatically present the patient with more questions that help form a more narrow diagnosis. A patient can then attach the results of this questionnaire when booking an appointment to help doctors better understand their patient’s concerns and decide on a more suitable treatment.
Prescription Management
The prescription management system acts as another service to create an excellent and convenient experience with medical telecommunications for both patient and doctor. Doctors have the ability to upload a patient’s prescription to their profile, including the medication, dosage, and other pertinent information. Each patient’s prescriptions will then be viewable to them from their personal devices in one accessible and organized location.
