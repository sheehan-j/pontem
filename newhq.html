New Company Headquarters Building
May 10th, 2021
With a company’s success comes an inherent need for expansion. Since our company’s founding, we have upgraded our software, optimized the user experience of our website, instated new leadership, and tweaked our fundamental ideas, values, and priorities. Pontem Medical has continued to expand, and we have collectively decided that our next step forward should be a large one - a new headquarters.

Our new headquarters building has been built and designed to facilitate Pontem Medical’s further growth and success. We have vastly expanded office spaces to make room for a multitude of new employees. With this space now available, we hope to bring on new thinkers, designers, and developers with progressive and innovative ideas to add to our company. Moreover, we have blocked off parts of the building for conference spaces (socially distanced, of course!). We hope to use these spaces for many more team meetings to encourage valuable discussions and debates and team-bonding, as well.

Finally, with a brand new base-of-operations, Pontem Medical took the opportunity to go fully zero-emissions. We care for the world around us, and this is how we’ve chosen to show it. Our building is powered completely by solar panels and wind energy from nearby turbines. Inside the building, we use completely recyclable utensils, plates and cups, and more, as well as an intricate recycling system.
