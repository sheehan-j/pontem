Our Company
Foundations, missions, and motivations.
Bridging the physical divide, virtually.
Pontem Medical’s mission is simple: bridge the physical gap that formed between doctor and patient. Whether it is due to location, restrictions, or danger, we work to provide a safe, efficient, and convenient virtual means of communication for medical purposes. The team at Pontem Medical puts the customer first as we design services with our core values in mind.
Our Values
Integrity
Integrity is something that is often lost in today’s competitive business environment. We choose to go against the grain, placing the well-being of the customer as our greatest priority. Pontem Medical acts with honesty, fairness, and dependability in all that we do.
Excellence
In everything we do, Pontem Medical strives to be the best it can. We create the best possible experience for customers using all of the knowledge and resources available. We work to stick out from the crowd with our sheer dedication and achievement of excellence.
Collaboration
We believe the incorporation of diverse ideas and perspectives is critical to success. To facilitate this, we schedule frequent company-wide for brainstorming, discussing, and debating new improvements. Moreover, we always take advice and critique from customers with great importance.
Company History
Pontem Medical’s years as a company may be few, but our major steps forward are not. Since our founding in 2016, we have developed a tradition and standard of excellence. We may honor and celebrate our milestones, but we do not see them as excuses to be complacent - we see them as motivation to continue working as hard as ever.
Company Founding
July 2017
In early 2017, our company’s founder was struggling to find time for a doctor's visit. It was just a regular check-up, but the drive was over 45 minutes, meaning he would have to block out over 2 hours of his day. This was the idea that would lead him to found Pontem Medical in July of 2017 to solve that exact issue.
1000 Customers
June 2019
To reach one thousand customers in under two years was an incredible milestone for Pontem Medical. One thousand different physician’s practices had a plan with our company; this fact motivated us to continue striving for excellence and creating the best possible experience for customers.
Software Revamp
November 2019
In November 2019, our company decided to make a significant improvement to our software/services. To take another step forward toward excellence, we revamped our software’s entire user interface, making the experience much more enjoyable for customers. We also made behind-the-scenes improvements to make the software faster with shorter load times.
New Company President
April 2020
With our services suddenly becoming more relevant than ever, Pontem Medical chose to select someone to serve in a new leadership position - Company President. To guide us through new and intimidating territory, we felt an added executive at the top of Pontem’s decision-making chain would be the best course of action.
New Company HQ
May 2021
Pontem Medical’s most recent major step forward was our move to a new headquarters building. With our recent massive growth as a company, a new and large space was needed. Now, our base of operations will facilitate even greater growth and innovation.
