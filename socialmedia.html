Follow Pontem Medical On Social Media
March 17th, 2021
Pontem Medical has recently launched social media accounts as another outlet of communication for our company. We are encouraging all of our customers to follow our Instagram and Twitter to stay in the loop!

On our social media pages, we will be posting frequent updates about any changes with our company or any important information we feel our customers should know about. Social media will also now act as a platform for customer service and help. Direct message us through Instagram or Twitter and a representative will respond to your issue or concern as soon as possible. Finally, and most important, we will be running promotions and discounts exclusive to our social media pages. Customers who begin a plan with us using an eligible code found on our social media page will receive a discount, and exclusive merchandise will be advertised and sold.
